Jun Mochizuki

Jun Mochizuki is best known as the author of Pandora Hearts and The Case Study of Vanitas. Mochizuki was born on December 22 in Kanagawa Prefecture. Her first work was a one-shot titled Pandora Hearts. Following this publication through Square Enix, she launched Crimson-Shell, serialized from Sep 2005 to Feb 2006. Following Crimson-Shell's completion, she launched Pandora Hearts, different from the previous oneshot.
